Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Simple Summary: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All price tracking data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Amazon Variation Price Tracker extension does not collect any personal information, including:

  • Your browsing history
  • Your Amazon account information
  • Your purchase history
  • Your search queries
  • Your IP address
  • Any personally identifiable information

2. Local Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's Storage API:

  • Product ASINs (Amazon product identifiers)
  • Product titles
  • Scanned price variations
  • Timestamps of scans

This data is stored solely for the purpose of displaying price history to you. It never leaves your device.

3. Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save price history locally on your device
  • activeTab: To read product information from Amazon pages you're viewing
  • amazon.com access: To scan and extract price data from Amazon product pages

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any third-party servers or services. All functionality runs entirely within your browser.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data because we don't collect any data in the first place.

6. Amazon.com

This extension interacts with Amazon.com by reading publicly available product information from pages you visit. The extension does not interfere with Amazon's functionality or violate their terms of service.

7. Future Changes to Monetization

Currently, this extension is completely free with no monetization. If we add affiliate links or other monetization in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified through the Chrome Web Store update process.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all stored price history by uninstalling the extension
  • View stored data by opening Chrome DevTools on the extension popup
  • Stop using the extension at any time

9. Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information at all, this is not a concern.
