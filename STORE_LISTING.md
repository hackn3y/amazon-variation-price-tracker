## Detailed Description

Stop overpaying for different colors! Find the cheapest Amazon product variation instantly.

**What It Does:**
Amazon often charges different prices for different colors, sizes, or styles of the same product. This extension automatically scans ALL variations and shows you which one costs the least, saving you time and money.

**Key Features:**
✓ Instant scanning of all product variations (colors, sizes, styles)
✓ Sorted results showing cheapest options first
✓ One-click purchase links to buy the best deal
✓ Price history tracking across multiple scans
✓ Fast performance - scans 24 variations in about 1 minute
✓ Clean, simple interface
✓ Works on all Amazon.com product pages with variations

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to any Amazon product page that has multiple color/size options
2. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
3. Click the "Scan All Variations" button
4. Wait while it checks all options (you'll see progress updates)
5. View sorted results with the cheapest variation highlighted
6. Click on any result to purchase that specific variation

**Perfect For:**
• Finding the cheapest color/size option before buying
• Comparing prices across all variations at once
• Tracking price changes over time
• Smart shopping on Amazon

**Privacy & Security:**
• No data collection - everything stays on your device
• No account required
• No tracking or analytics
• Open source code available on GitHub

**Supported Site:**
• Amazon.com (US)

**Note:** This extension helps you find better deals but does not modify Amazon prices or interfere with Amazon's functionality. All prices shown are Amazon's actual prices.

Save money on every Amazon purchase - install now!

This extension's single purpose is to help users identify the lowest-priced color, size, or style variation of Amazon products by automatically scanning and comparing all available options on product pages.

## Permission Justifications

**storage**
Purpose: To save price history and scan results locally on the user's device
Usage: Stores product ASINs, titles, prices, and timestamps for displaying historical price data to users

**activeTab**
Purpose: To interact with the current Amazon product page the user is viewing
Usage: Reads product information (title, price, variations) from the active Amazon tab to perform price comparison

**Host Permission: https://www.amazon.com/***
Purpose: Extension only works on Amazon.com product pages
Usage: Content script needs access to Amazon DOM to extract product data and variation information
